# CORE — Board Advisors Management
> **Advisor não é decoração no deck — é ferramenta operacional quando bem usado.**

## Responsabilidades
- Advisor identification + recruitment
- Advisor agreement (scope, compensation, term, exclusivity)
- Cadência (calls mensais, bimestrais)
- Tracking ROI por advisor (sem mensurar, vira vaidade)
- Renewal / off-boarding decisions
- Strategic value vs status value distinction

## Frameworks
- **Advisor compensation**: 0.1-1% equity vesting 2-4 anos
- **Advisor scope clear**: specific expertise (não "generic strategic")
- **Quarterly advisor review** (utility check)
- **a16z advisor playbook**

## Casos típicos
- Empresa nova: 2-3 advisors core
- Series A: refresh advisory board
- Specific expertise hire (ex: regulação setor, fundraising US, M&A)
- Off-board advisor que parou de agregar
- Founder advisor → board member transition

## Heurísticas
- **2-4 advisors ativos**: além disso, virou diretório
- **0.25-0.5% equity por advisor** em pre-Seed; 0.1-0.25% Series A+
- **Vesting 4 anos** (mesmo de employees)
- **Termination clause clear**: 30-90 dias notice
- **Status advisor (nome no deck) ≠ working advisor**: separar
- **Calls mensais ou off-board**: sem cadência, sem valor

---

## Pipeline CHRO (9 fases)

### Fase 1 — Org design + headcount plan
Owner: `chro` + cross `ceo` (estratégia) + `cfo` (budget)
- Org chart atual + futuro (6/12/18 meses)
- Hire plan (quem contratar quando)
- Output: `_Areas/CHRO/org-design.md`

### Fase 2 — Recruiting
Owner: `chro-recruiting`
- Job descriptions
- Sourcing (LinkedIn, indicação, agências, comunidades)
- Screening + interview process
- Offer + closing
- Output: `_Areas/CHRO/recruiting-pipeline.md`

### Fase 3 — Onboarding
Owner: `chro-people-ops`
- Day 0 (welcome + setup tech + docs)
- Week 1 (cultura + processos + ritmo)
- Month 1 (check-in + feedback)
- Month 3 (review fim do experiência period)
- Output: `_Areas/CHRO/onboarding-playbook.md`

### Fase 4 — Comp & Benefits
Owner: `chro-people-ops` + cross `cfo`
- Comp bands (faixas por nível + role)
- Benefits package (VR/VA, saúde, vesting equity)
- Periodicidade de revisão (anual)
- Output: `_Areas/CHRO/comp-bands.xlsx`

### Fase 5 — Performance management
Owner: `chro` + `chro-learning-development`
- Performance reviews (quarterly check-ins + anual)
- 9-box (performance × potential)
- PIP (Performance Improvement Plan) quando aplicável
- Output: `_Areas/CHRO/performance-cycles-YYYY.md`

### Fase 6 — L&D (Learning & Development)
Owner: `chro-learning-development`
- Career paths por função
- Training programs
- Mentorship pairings
- Output: `_Areas/CHRO/ld-program.md`

### Fase 7 — Culture + comunicação interna
Owner: `chro-culture`
- Rituais (all-hands semanal/mensal, retro, town hall)
- Valores explícitos + comportamentos esperados
- Pulse survey (eNPS, engagement)
- Output: `_Areas/CHRO/culture-playbook.md`

### Fase 8 — Off-boarding
Owner: `chro-people-ops` + cross `clo-trabalhista`
- Demissão (com/sem causa)
- Knowledge transfer
- Equity decisions (vesting acceleration?)
- Exit interview
- Output: `_Areas/CHRO/offboarding-playbook.md`

### Fase 9 — Reporting
Owner: `chro` (chief)
- Headcount + turnover + engagement
- Input pra `board-pack-builder`

---

## Princípio inviolável

**Hire slow, fire fast** (Paul Graham). Hire errado custa 3-6 meses. Demora pra ter certeza no início, age rápido quando errar.

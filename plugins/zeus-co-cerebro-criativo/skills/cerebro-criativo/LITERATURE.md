# LITERATURE — Cérebro Criativo

> Bibliografia curada em 4 camadas. Skill consulta esta lista periodicamente (cron semanal atualiza com lançamentos relevantes — princípio "cada LEP se atualiza sozinho").

## Camada 1 — Fundação científica (obrigatório)

### Neurociência da criatividade
- **Brandt, A. & Eagleman, D.** (2017). *The Runaway Species: How Human Creativity Remakes the World.* Catapult. — base operacional Bend/Break/Blend.
- **Eagleman, D.** (2020). *Livewired: The Inside Story of the Ever-Changing Brain.* Pantheon — plasticidade neural.
- **Kounios, J. & Beeman, M.** (2015). *The Eureka Factor: Aha Moments, Creative Insight, and the Brain.* Random House.
- **Csikszentmihalyi, M.** (1996). *Creativity: Flow and the Psychology of Discovery and Invention.* HarperCollins.

### Cognitive science of creativity
- **Boden, M.** (2004). *The Creative Mind: Myths and Mechanisms* (2nd ed.). Routledge. — taxonomia 3 tipos.
- **Boden, M.** (2009). "Computer Models of Creativity." *AI Magazine* 30(3).
- **Sawyer, R. K.** (2012). *Explaining Creativity: The Science of Human Innovation* (2nd ed.). Oxford UP.

### Conceptual blending
- **Fauconnier, G. & Turner, M.** (2002). *The Way We Think: Conceptual Blending and the Mind's Hidden Complexities.* Basic Books.
- **Turner, M.** (2014). *The Origin of Ideas: Blending, Creativity, and the Human Spark.* Oxford UP.
- **Pereira, F.** (2007). *Creativity and Artificial Intelligence: A Conceptual Blending Approach.* Mouton de Gruyter.

### Analogy & FARG (Hofstadter school)
- **Hofstadter, D.** (1979). *Gödel, Escher, Bach.* Basic Books.
- **Hofstadter, D. & FARG** (1995). *Fluid Concepts and Creative Analogies.* Basic Books.
- **Mitchell, M.** (1993). *Analogy-Making as Perception.* MIT Press.
- **Hofstadter, D. & Sander, E.** (2013). *Surfaces and Essences: Analogy as the Fuel and Fire of Thinking.* Basic Books.

### Conceptual spaces & semantic distance
- **Gärdenfors, P.** (2000). *Conceptual Spaces: The Geometry of Thought.* MIT Press.
- **Gärdenfors, P.** (2014). *The Geometry of Meaning: Semantics Based on Conceptual Spaces.* MIT Press.
- **Beaty, R. E. & Johnson, D. R.** (2021). "Automating creativity assessment with SemDis." *Behavior Research Methods*, 53, 757-780.
- **Beaty et al.** (2023). "Beyond Semantic Distance: Automated Scoring of Divergent Thinking Greatly Improves with Large Language Models." *ScienceDirect*.

### Generative/Exploratory creativity
- **Finke, R. A., Ward, T. B. & Smith, S. M.** (1992). *Creative Cognition: Theory, Research, and Applications.* MIT Press. — Geneplore model.

## Camada 2 — Frameworks operacionais (aplicação)

### Ideation methods
- **Eberle, B.** (1971). *SCAMPER: Games for Imagination Development.* — operacionalização 7 lentes.
- **Osborn, A. F.** (1953). *Applied Imagination.* — fundação brainstorming + SCAMPER.
- **de Bono, E.** (1985). *Six Thinking Hats.* — paralelo.
- **de Bono, E.** (1992). *Serious Creativity.* — lateral thinking.
- **Michalko, M.** (2006). *Thinkertoys.* — toolkit técnicas.
- **Kelley, T. & Kelley, D.** (2013). *Creative Confidence.* IDEO — design thinking aplicado.

### Brand & narrative
- **Heath, C. & Heath, D.** (2007). *Made to Stick.* — SUCCES framework.
- **Heath, C. & Heath, D.** (2010). *Switch.* — change framework.
- **Pinkott, R.** (2005). *A Whole New Mind.* — high concept, high touch.
- **Aaker, D.** (1996). *Building Strong Brands.* — brand foundation.
- **Holt, D.** (2004). *How Brands Become Icons.* — cultural branding.
- **Aaker, J.** (2005). *Spinning Gold.* — narrative strategy.

### Story & screenplay (aplicável a marca/conceito)
- **McKee, R.** (1997). *Story: Substance, Structure, Style.* — story arc.
- **Campbell, J.** (1949). *The Hero with a Thousand Faces.* — monomyth.
- **Lakoff, G. & Johnson, M.** (1980). *Metaphors We Live By.* — frames.
- **Lakoff, G.** (2004). *Don't Think of an Elephant.* — reframing.

### Strategy / business creativity
- **Kim, W. C. & Mauborgne, R.** (2005). *Blue Ocean Strategy.* — value innovation = Bend/Break/Blend aplicado a estratégia.
- **Christensen, C.** (1997). *The Innovator's Dilemma.* — disruption framework.
- **Christensen, C. et al.** (2016). *Competing Against Luck.* — Jobs to Be Done.
